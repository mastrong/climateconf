Registration Information

General information

Registration for the Visualising Climate Conference 2026 is secured via payment of the appropriate registration fee. There is no waiving of registration fees except for scientists receiving support.

The fee includes participation in the onsite meeting as well as lunch and coffee breaks. Participants are responsible for their own travel and accommodation arrangements and expenses.

Please read our instructions for online registration as well as the terms and conditions.

In accordance with current EU sanctions, we will not accept any payment of registration, abstract processing, or other fees from Russian or Belarusian institutions or banks paid on behalf of researchers based at or affiliated with them.

Rates for participation

  • Early registration rates provide three payment options: credit card, PayPal, or bank transfer.
  • Early registration rates apply until 1 June 2026.
  • Standard registration rates apply after the early registration deadline until 18 October 2026, 23:59 CEST.
  • Onsite payments are not possible. All registrations must be processed and paid via the online registration form.

Letter of Invitation

Participants who require an official letter of invitation for visa purposes, to secure travel funding, or for administrative reasons should request this document no later than 15 September 2026 by completing the conference registration. Requests received after this date may not be processed by embassies.

This invitation does not imply any financial support from the organisers. The organisers cannot provide or sign any EVE documents or guarantee letters. The invitation will only cover the period of the Visualising Climate 2026 Conference (4â€“6 November 2026) and the day or days of travel.

A letter of invitation can only be issued to abstract authors and or registered participants with a paid conference registration. Letters for accompanying persons, including family members, cannot be provided.

Instructions for online registration

  • Please ensure you have read and understood the cancellation policy and registration conditions provided on this website.
  • All information should be entered using upper and lower case letters, for example Jane Smith.
  • For participants from EU countries, a valid VAT ID of the institution is required for invoicing purposes. Affiliations based in Italy are subject to Italian VAT regulations.
  • You will have the opportunity to review and edit your registration details before completing payment.
  • The maximum time allowed to complete the registration form is 60 minutes before the system times out.
  • The latest versions of Firefox, Google Chrome, Safari, and Edge are recommended for optimal use of the forms.

Data Protection Notice

In accordance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (General Data Protection Regulation, GDPR) and applicable national laws, personal data provided during the registration process for Visualising Climate 2026 will be processed under the conditions outlined below.

Data Controller and Joint Controllers:
The registration fee will be managed by the University of Miami, which acts as the primary data controller. Personal data will also be shared with the organising partners Copernicus-ECMWF, CSIC, BSC, and BC3 solely for managing participation in the conference and related activities.

Purpose of processing:
Data will be used exclusively for registration management, communication regarding the event, and logistical coordination.

International data transfer:
As the University of Miami is located in the United States, personal data will be transferred outside the European Economic Area in compliance with GDPR requirements, based on explicit consent.

Legal basis:
Processing is based on consent provided by completing and submitting the registration form.

Data subject rights:
Rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, and portability may be exercised at any time by sending a written request, accompanied by a copy of an official identification document, to hello@visualisingclimate.com.

Additional information:
For further details, please consult our Privacy Policy.

By accepting this form, you give explicit consent for the processing and international transfer of personal data for the purposes stated above.
