Privacy Policy

Visualising Climate 2026

1. Who we are

Visualising Climate 2026 is organised by Copernicus-ECMWF, University of Miami, CSIC, BSC, and BC3. The registration process is managed by the University of Miami, which acts as the primary data controller.

2. What data we collect

  • Identification data (name, email, affiliation, country)
  • Billing information (for payment processing)
  • Preferences related to participation (sessions, workshops)

3. Purpose of processing

  • Manage your registration and participation in the conference
  • Communicate important updates about the event
  • Issue invoices and process payments

4. Legal basis

Processing is based on your consent and, where applicable, contractual necessity for registration.

5. Data sharing

Your data will be shared with the organising partners (Copernicus-ECMWF, CSIC, BSC, BC3) for event management purposes only. We do not sell or share your data for marketing purposes.

6. International data transfer

As the University of Miami is located in the United States, your data will be transferred outside the EU/EEA. This transfer is based on your explicit consent and will follow GDPR requirements.

7. Data retention

Your data will be retained for 12 months after the end of the conference, unless legal obligations require longer storage.

8. Your rights

You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, object, and request portability of your data. To exercise these rights, contact hello@visualisingclimate.com .

9. Security measures

We implement technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including encrypted payment systems and restricted access.

10. Contact

For any questions regarding data protection, please email hello@visualisingclimate.com .
